AI governance

Compliance built in, not bolted on

Every VertexStat AI deployment includes enterprise governance controls aligned to HIPAA, FFIEC, SOC 2, and your internal risk frameworks.

🔐
Role-based access control
Every user sees only the data their role permits. No broad data access, ever.
👁
Read-only data views
AI agents query approved read-only views. No writes, no mutations, no bulk exports.
📋
Full audit logging
Every query, agent action, and response is logged with user, timestamp, and data source.
🛡
PII protection & masking
Sensitive identifiers are masked at the data layer before AI processing. HIPAA-aligned.
✋
Human approval workflows
High-risk actions require explicit human confirmation before execution.
📊
Confidence scoring
Every AI answer includes a confidence score and source citation so users know when to verify.
